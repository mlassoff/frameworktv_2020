Watch Framework Television on your Roku Device

Through the web

1. Make sure you are signed in to the Roku web site through your Roku Account.
2. Visit https://channelstore.roku.com/details/575757/framework-television to view the Framework Channel Page.
3. Click the “Add Channel” button.

Through Your Roku Television or Roku Device

1. Using your Roku remote navigate to the Search option from the main menu 2. Using the on-screen keyboard type the letters “Framework”. You’ll eventually see the entry “Framework Television” appear in the list on the right. 3. Select “Framework Television” from the list on the right and then select the “Add Channel” option on the subsequent screen.
